Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

What We Collect

When you sign in with Google or GitHub, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. We use this solely to authenticate you and personalize your experience.

How We Use Your Data

  • Chat messages are stored in your session and are only accessible to you.
  • Files you create in the sandbox are stored per-user and are not shared.
  • Google Drive access (if you mount it) is used only to read/write files you explicitly request. We do not access your Drive in the background.

Third-Party Services

  • LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) process your chat messages to generate responses. Messages are sent per their respective privacy policies.
  • Sentry is used for error monitoring. It may collect anonymized error data and browser metadata.
  • Cloudflare hosts the application. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete individual sessions or all your data through the app's settings.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
